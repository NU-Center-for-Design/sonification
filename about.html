About

The Sonification Archive is an initiative of Center for Design at Northeastern University in collaboration with the Deusto Design Research Group.

The archive is curated by Sara Lenzi and Paolo Ciuccarelli Center for Design, Northeastern University
with the assistance of Houjiang Liu from University of Texas at Austin, PhD in Information Science.

How to Use the Canvas

  1. Use Case: Define who your users are, what the goals and the context of your sonification are.
  2. Mapping Choices: Define how you map data parameters to sound parameters.
  3. Sonification Approach: Find out the position of your approach to the sonification.
  4. Listening Experience: Imagine how your users will listen to the sonification.
