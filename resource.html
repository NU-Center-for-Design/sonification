How to Use the Canvas

  1. Use Case: Define who your users are, what the goals and the context of your sonification are.
  2. Mapping Choices: Define how you map data parameters to sound parameters.
  3. Sonification Approach: Find out the position of your approach to the sonification.
  4. Listening Experience: Imagine how your users will listen to the sonification.
